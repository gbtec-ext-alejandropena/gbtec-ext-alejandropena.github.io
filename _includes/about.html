About me

I've been working professionally in software development since approximately 2011. But it's a whole life that I've been an enthusiast of technologies... since I was very young.

I'm a person with a technical profile, after all it is what I like the most, but I have also collaborated in tasks related to project management. Currently I work in the development team of BIC Cloud Workflow & BIC Cloud GRC. An integral solution for the design and automation of workflow processes, and its implementation for the management of controls and risks.

I really enjoy what I do, and that encourages me to keep improving everyday so as to become a better professional.
