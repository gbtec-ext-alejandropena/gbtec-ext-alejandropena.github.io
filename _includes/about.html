About me

I have also done consulting work related to this document manager, participating in performance audits and technical training to IT departments.

Currently, I have moved to Pittsburgh (USA) with Sngular. Here I'm building from scratch an application to give stock exchange real-time information to PNC Bank customers based on Kafka and Confluent.

I really enjoy what I do, and that encourages me to keep improving every day so as to become a better professional.
