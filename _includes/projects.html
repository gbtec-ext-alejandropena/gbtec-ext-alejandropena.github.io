Smart Government

Angular 5, Java, Spring Boot, Docker, PostgreSQL, Nuxeo...

The project aims to improve the efficiency of cities through information and communication technologies. The use of information collected through different means will allow the Administration to make optimal decisions and better use the assets of the city, as well as offer new services that adapt to their real demands. Smart Government goes beyond the capture of this data, since its mission is to apply artificial intelligence systems that interpret and relate all that information, to reach conclusions that could hardly reach a person.
